Download & Sizes

Three PNG sizes: 200×200 px (email footers, website thumbnails), 500×500 px (business cards, flyers, A4 posters), and 1000×1000 px (banners, signage, packaging). All are watermark-free and free to download.
Use the 500px download for business cards and A4 flyers. Use the 1000px download for banners, signage, or packaging. As a rule of thumb, the minimum printable size is 2×2 cm (≈0.8 in). Always maintain a quiet zone (white border) of at least 4 module widths around the code to ensure reliable scanning.
Currently PNG downloads are supported (up to 1000×1000 px). SVG export is on our roadmap. For large-format print from the 1000px PNG, the file will be sharp when scaled up in design software like Illustrator or Canva.
