How to Create a Restaurant Menu QR Code

You need a public URL for your menu first — here are the easiest options.

1

Upload Your Menu Online

Use Google Drive (upload PDF → get shareable link), Canva (publish as website), or link to your Zomato/Swiggy/Dotpe page.

2

Copy the URL

Copy the public link to your menu. Make sure it opens without needing a login.

3

Generate the QR Code

Open our generator, paste the URL in the URL tab, and customise the colours to match your brand.

4

Print & Laminate

Download at 1000px. Print one per table and laminate for durability. Add a "Scan for Menu" label.

Want to update the menu later? Since the QR code links to your URL, just update the PDF or page at that URL — your QR code automatically shows the new menu without any reprinting.

Why Restaurants Are Switching to QR Menus

  • 💰
    Save on printing costs
    No more reprinting menus every time prices change or items are added. Update the digital menu once.
  • 🦠
    Contactless & hygienic
    Guests don't touch a shared physical menu. Better hygiene, especially appreciated post-pandemic.
  • 📱
    Works on every smartphone
    No app download required. The camera app on any iPhone or Android opens the menu automatically.
  • 🌐
    Works offline too
    The QR code itself requires no internet. Only the linked menu page needs connectivity.
