QR Tiger — Best for Enterprise, Expensive for Everyone Else

QR Tiger (qrcode-tiger.com)

Verdict: Feature-rich but heavily paywalled. Free tier is very limited.

QR Tiger is one of the most feature-complete QR code platforms — offering 20+ QR types, bulk generation, Zapier integration, and detailed analytics. However, most of these features are locked behind paid plans starting at $7/month.

What's free: Basic static QR codes with limited customisation. High-res download is available.

What's paywalled: Dynamic QR codes, analytics, bulk generation, logo overlay, and most QR types beyond the basics.

  • ✅ 20+ QR types
  • ✅ Bulk generation (3,000 at once)
  • ❌ Dynamic QR codes require subscription
  • ❌ QR codes expire if you cancel
  • ❌ No UPI payment QR type
  • ❌ Requires account creation

Best for: Enterprise marketing teams with budget for analytics and bulk generation.

QRCode Monkey — Best Free Option With Design Features

QRCode Monkey (qrcode-monkey.com)

Verdict: Genuinely one of the best free options — generous free tier, no watermark.

QRCode Monkey is our closest peer in the "free with no watermark" category. It offers logo upload, custom shapes, and high-res downloads for free. The interface is more complex than ours but powerful.

What's free: PNG + SVG download at high resolution, logo overlay, custom eye shapes, 15+ QR types, no watermark.

What's paywalled: Dynamic QR codes, analytics.

  • ✅ Free logo overlay (advantage over us)
  • ✅ No watermark, no account needed
  • ✅ SVG + PDF + PNG exports free
  • ❌ No UPI payment QR type
  • ❌ No live preview while typing
  • ❌ Slower interface (server-side generation)
  • ❌ Sends your data to their servers

Best for: Designers who need logo overlay and PDF export and are comfortable with a more complex interface.

QR Code Generator by Bitly — Most Restrictive "Free" Tier

QR Code Generator (qr-code-generator.com)

Verdict: Heavy limitations on the free tier. Watermark on downloads, no custom colours without paying.

This tool markets itself heavily but the free tier is significantly restricted. Custom colours, high-resolution downloads, and removing the watermark all require a paid plan ($5/month+).

  • ❌ Watermark on free downloads
  • ❌ Custom colours require payment
  • ❌ High-res PNG requires payment
  • ❌ Requires account to save QR codes
  • ✅ Dynamic QR codes available (paid)
  • ✅ Good analytics dashboard (paid)

Best for: Businesses that need dynamic QR codes with analytics and don't mind paying. Not recommended for free use.

GoQR.me — Lightweight and Privacy-Conscious

GoQR.me

Verdict: Truly free, but very basic. No custom colours, poor UX, low-resolution output.

GoQR.me is one of the oldest free QR generators and is completely free with no account required. But it shows its age — no custom colours, no live preview, and maximum download size is too small for print.

  • ✅ Completely free, no account
  • ✅ API available
  • ❌ No custom colours
  • ❌ Low resolution output (max 1000px but no customisation)
  • ❌ No WhatsApp, UPI, or modern QR types
  • ❌ Outdated UI, no live preview

Best for: Developers who want a quick API endpoint. Not suitable for design-conscious users.
