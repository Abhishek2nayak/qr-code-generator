PhonePe UPI ID Formats

Your PhonePe UPI ID depends on which bank is linked. Common formats:

  • 9876543210@ybl — Yes Bank (most common PhonePe default)
  • name@axisbank — Axis Bank linked account
  • name@ibl — IndusInd Bank linked account
  • name@icici — ICICI Bank linked via PhonePe

Always check your active UPI ID in the PhonePe app before creating the QR code.
