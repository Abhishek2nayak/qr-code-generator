vCard QR Code vs NFC Business Cards

NFC cards (like Dot cards or Linq) are popular, but a vCard QR code has clear advantages:

  • ✅Works on all phones — iPhones support NFC only for Apple Wallet. QR codes work everywhere.
  • ✅No hardware cost — NFC cards cost ₹500–₹2000. QR codes are free.
  • ✅Printable anywhere — Add to business cards, email signatures, LinkedIn background, and more.
  • ✅Works offline — No app or internet needed to save the contact after scanning.
