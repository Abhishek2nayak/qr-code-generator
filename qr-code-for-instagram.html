How to Make an Instagram QR Code in 3 Steps

No special Instagram QR tool needed — a URL QR code pointing to your profile works perfectly.

1

Copy Your Instagram URL

Go to instagram.com/yourusername in a browser and copy the full URL.

2

Paste Into the Generator

Open our generator, select the URL tab, and paste your Instagram profile URL.

3

Customise & Download

Choose brand colours (try purple or pink to match Instagram's palette) and download at 1000px for print.

💡 Pro Tip: Use a Linktree Instead

If you want one QR code for Instagram and other links (YouTube, WhatsApp, website), create a Linktree or Beacons page and encode that URL instead. One QR code, multiple links.
