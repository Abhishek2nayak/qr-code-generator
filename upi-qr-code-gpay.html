How to Create Your GPay QR Code

4 steps. Under 2 minutes. Works for personal and business UPI IDs.

1

Find Your UPI ID

Open Google Pay → tap your photo → Bank Account. Your UPI ID looks like name@okicici or phone@ybl.

2

Open the UPI Tab

Click the button above or go to the generator and select the UPI Payment tab.

3

Enter Your Details

Paste your UPI ID, enter your name, and optionally set a fixed amount (leave blank for variable).

4

Download & Display

Download at 1000px. Print on A5 paper, laminate it, and place it at your counter.
